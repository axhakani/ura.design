About

Ura is a design studio which helps Open Source and Internet Freedom projects with tailored branding, user experience design and usability research. We are based in Albania and Kosovo but operate internationally and (mostly) remotely. Our mission is to increase usability of decentralized, independent, and privacy-enhancing technologies.
